References for STRFPAK's Calculation

For a better understanding of the theory and practice of STRF estimation, please consult the following papers.
  • Theunissen, F. E., David, S. V., Singh, N. C., Hsu, A., Vinje, W. and Gallant, J. L. (2001) ``Estimating spatio-temporal receptive fields of auditory and visual neurons from their responses to natural stimuli'', Network: Comp. Neural Syst. 12, 1-28.
  • David, S. V.,Vinje, W. and Gallant, J. L., ``Natural Stimulus Statistics Alter the Receptive Field Structure of V1 Neurons'', (in progress).
  • D.J. Thomson and A.D. Chave, ``Jackknifed error estimates for spectra, coherence and transfer functions'', Advances in Spectrum Analysis and Array Processing vol 1, ed S Haykin (Upper Saddle River, NJ:Prentice-Hall).
