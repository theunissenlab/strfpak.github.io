Glossary

  • STRFPAK: STRFPAK is the nickname of our Spatial Temporal Receptive Fields Estimation Software Package.
  • Preprocessing: A transformation of the stimulus to put it into a form more amenable to computing a STRF. For example, calculating a spectrogram from a .wav file is a way of preprocessing data into a format more amenable to STRF calculations.
  • Post-processing: Any calculation that comes after the estimation of the STRF, the prediction of neural response and the validation of the STRF. It usually consists of some form of analysis of the results sets, such as clustering of all the result parameters.
  • Prediction: One application of the estimated STRF is to predict the neural response with different stimuli data provided.
  • Validation: The stage after prediction, which measures how good the estimated STRF is.
  • Experiment: A term used to describe the entire set from which results are derived. An experiment can consist of several trials over different days, each of which may have several runs.
  • Frame: The 2D spatial data available at each time-point.
